Neighborhood Lowdown

This is the neighborhood that truly has it all. Southeast enough to be close to the bars in LES, but not south enough that you're mingling with the NYU students, Flatiron is also located relatively close to the Meatpacking party scene. Its lucky inhabitants are close to a Whole Foods, Trader Joes, and multiple subway lines. The shops are uniquely housed in old buildings and tons of casual restaurants to try out too.

Public Transit Service: N,Q,R,4,5,6

Cup of Joe

Stumptown Coffee - 20 W 29th St. (29th & Broadway)

Their coffee is simply amazing! You must go!

Eataly
#europe #pasta #market #beer #empireview

A fun trip to Europe, Eataly is a market that stocks up on all the goods you could ever want from Italy. It's multiple restaurants housed inside vary from a fancy fish and steak sit down to made-to-order charcuterie boards. La Bierreria, its rooftop Eatery, offers good beer and a view of the Empire State Building.

Drink

230 5th
#rooftop #loungemusic #midtownview

Le Bain at the top of the Standard Hotel - My favorite view and everything you would imagine a typical NYC nightclub to be. Even clubs are not really your scene, I would recommend going to the the outdoor portion just to have a look at the breathtaking night view, especially since you'll be there in summer!

Flatiron Park
#nature #park #cozy #burger

A cozy and nice local park, perfect for a relaxing afternoon on a beautiful day. If you want to try a famous ShakeShack burger, you can do it here. Lines are long as usual, to the extent (and this isn't verified!) that I heard there is a video camera of the line, which live streams on the web so you can go during periods of less rush.

Recommended Trip

Start off your morning with a cup of coffee from Stumptown Coffee Roasters.

Shop to your heart's content at the various and unique stores around the Flatiron/Union Square area. This might just be something I like to do, but there is a Barnes and Nobles bookstore that is great to spend a couple hours browsing in when you want some time to yourself!

Relax and enjoy the night view at La Bierreria, Eataly's rooftop restaurant.
