Neighborhood Lowdown

Hands down my favorite neighborhood in Manhattan. The beautiful brownstones line up on the side of the streets and are adorned with trees on trees - something you'll find that is rare and valuable in NYC. There are still small cobblestone streets, which make the space feel intimate even though you are in the heart of one of the largest cities.

Public Transit Service: 1,2,3,A,C,E

Let's get started

Eat

Rosemary's
#pasta #homegrown #foccacia #detaileddecor

This is a random place I found one night with a friend, but the quality of this casual Italian eatery surprised me. They actually grow part of the food that they serve on their outdoor rooftop and I love the intricacy of the decor inside.

Tartine
#european #outdoor

A popular brunch spot that has both indoor and outdoor seating. I haven't been here before because I've never been able to bring myself to wait in the long line that usually adorns the entrance, but I've always been envious of the happy brunchers with their delicious food.

Magnolia
#cupcakes

Magnolia Cupcakes can be found in locations throughout the city, but this is their most well known location. Good go to place for any special occasions or just to enjoy a tasty, not too sweet cupcake!

Drink

Spotted Pig
#brunch #drinks #dinner #burgers

I forget why this place is famous, but I think it may have been something about Bill Cliton's go-to place for a hamburger? Anyway, a cool spot to check out for any meal-brunch, drinks, dinner!
